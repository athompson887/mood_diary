# Mood Diary

## Track Your Emotions. Discover Yourself.

Mood Diary is a beautifully designed app that helps you understand your emotional patterns and improve your mental wellbeing.

---

## Features

### Daily Mood Tracking

Log your mood with simple, intuitive ratings. Choose from a range of emotions and see how you're feeling at a glance.

### Add Context

Capture what's on your mind with notes. Select from activities that influenced your day - exercise, work, socializing, and more.

### Visual Insights

Beautiful charts show your mood trends over time. Spot patterns and understand what affects your wellbeing.

### Calendar History

Browse your emotional journey in a clean calendar view. Tap any day to see your full entry.

### Accessible Design

- High contrast mode for better visibility
- Dyslexia-friendly font options (Lexend)
- Large tap targets
- Screen reader support
- Reduce motion option

### Privacy First

All your data stays on your device. No accounts. No cloud sync. No data collection. Your thoughts remain private.
