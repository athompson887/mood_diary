# Mood Diary Support

## Frequently Asked Questions

### General

**Q: How do I log my mood?**
A: Tap the + button on the home screen. Select your mood rating, optionally add notes and activities, then tap Save.

**Q: Where is my data stored?**
A: All data is stored locally on your device. Nothing is sent to external servers.

**Q: Can I sync my data across devices?**
A: Currently, Mood Diary stores data only on your device. Cloud sync may be added in a future update.

**Q: How do I delete my data?**
A: You can delete all data by uninstalling the app or clearing app data in your device settings.

### Premium Features

**Q: What do I get with Premium?**
A: Premium unlocks extended history, advanced statistics, data export, and removes any limitations.

**Q: How do I restore my purchase?**
A: Go to Settings > Restore Purchases. Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID or Google account used for the original purchase.

**Q: Can I get a refund?**
A: Refunds are handled by Apple (iOS) or Google (Android). Please contact them directly through their respective app stores.

### Troubleshooting

**Q: The app is crashing**
A: Try these steps:
1. Force close the app and reopen it
2. Restart your device
3. Update to the latest version
4. If issues persist, contact support

**Q: My data disappeared**
A: Data may be lost if:
- The app was uninstalled
- App data was cleared in settings
- A device reset was performed

Unfortunately, we cannot recover lost data as it's only stored on your device.

**Q: Premium features aren't working**
A: Try:
1. Go to Settings > Restore Purchases
2. Make sure you have an internet connection
3. Verify you're using the same account that made the purchase
