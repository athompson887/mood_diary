# Privacy Policy for Mood Diary

**Last Updated: December 14, 2025**

## Introduction

Mood Diary ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data when you use our mobile application.

## Data Collection

Mood Diary is designed with privacy as a core principle. We **do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data** to external servers.

### Local Data Storage

All data generated through your use of the app is stored **locally on your device only**:

- Mood entries and emotional ratings
- Journal notes and reflections
- Activities associated with moods
- Mood history and patterns
- Statistics and insights
- App preferences and settings
- Theme and accessibility preferences

### Cloud Features (Premium)

If you subscribe to Mood Diary Pro and enable cloud sync:

- Data is encrypted and synced securely
- You control what data is synced
- You can delete cloud data at any time
- Cloud sync is optional and can be disabled

## Data We Do Not Collect

We do **not** collect:

- Personal identification information
- Location data
- Device identifiers for tracking
- Health data beyond what you enter in the app
- Marketing or advertising data
- Your mood data on our servers

## Third-Party Services

### RevenueCat (Subscriptions)

If you purchase a premium subscription, we use RevenueCat to manage subscriptions:

- RevenueCat processes subscription status only
- No personal mood data is shared with RevenueCat
- See [RevenueCat's Privacy Policy](https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy)

### Google Fonts

The app may download fonts from Google Fonts for accessibility features:

- This is a one-time download cached locally
- No personal data is sent to Google
- See [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

## Data Security

Since all mood data remains on your device:

- Your data is protected by your device's security features (passcode, Face ID, Touch ID, fingerprint)
- Data is automatically backed up via iCloud/Google backup if you have device backups enabled
- Deleting the app will remove all associated data from your device

## Children's Privacy

Mood Diary does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. The app is intended for users who want to track their own emotional wellbeing.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

- Access all your data (stored locally on your device)
- Delete all your data (by deleting the app or using in-app data management)
- Export your data (feature planned for future release)

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the app and on this page with an updated revision date.
