Mood Diary is a beautifully designed app that helps you track your daily emotions and understand your mental wellbeing patterns.

TRACK YOUR MOOD
Log how you're feeling with simple, intuitive mood ratings. Add notes to capture what's on your mind and select activities that influenced your day.

DISCOVER PATTERNS
View your mood history in a beautiful calendar view. Spot trends and patterns with visual statistics that help you understand what affects your wellbeing.

GAIN INSIGHTS
See weekly and monthly mood averages, track your most common activities, and identify what helps you feel your best.

ACCESSIBLE BY DESIGN
- High contrast mode for better visibility
- Dyslexia-friendly font options
- Large tap targets for easier interaction
- Works with screen readers

PRIVACY FIRST
All your data stays on your device. No accounts required, no data sent to servers. Your thoughts remain private.
