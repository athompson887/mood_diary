Thank you for reviewing Mood Diary.

This is a mood tracking app that stores all data locally on the device. No account or login is required.

To test the app:
1. Launch the app
2. Tap the + button to add a mood entry
3. Select a mood rating, add optional notes and activities
4. View your entries in the History tab
5. See statistics in the Statistics tab

Premium features can be tested via the Settings > Upgrade option.

Please let us know if you have any questions.
